Yogurt & Sausage Breakfast Sandwich

Recipe Details

  • Time Needed: 20 minutes
  • Difficulty Level: 2
  • Number of Portions: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread from the loaf
  • 4 cheese slices
  • 4 sausages from the package
  • 4 tablespoons of Hermanns mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons of yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of butter from the dish
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard from the bottle
  • 4 eggs from the carton

Instructions

  1. Heat a skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter.

  2. Crack 4 eggs into the skillet and cook until the whites are set but yolks are still runny (about 5 minutes). Remove from heat and set aside.

  3. In the same skillet, cook the sausages until they are browned and cooked through (about 8 minutes), then remove from heat.

  4. In a small bowl, mix Hermanns mayonnaise with 2 tablespoons of yogurt and 1 tablespoon of mustard.

  5. Toast the bread slices under a grill or in a toaster.

  6. Spread the mayonnaise-yogurt-mustard mixture on one side of each toasted bread slice.

  7. Place a cheese slice on each slice of bread.

  8. Add a sausage and an egg on top of the cheese.

  9. Close each sandwich with another slice of bread, slice in half if desired, and serve warm.

Enjoy your delicious Yogurt & Sausage Breakfast Sandwich!
