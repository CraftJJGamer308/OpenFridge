Savory Sausage & Cheese Sandwich with Mustard Yogurt Spread

Recipe Details

  • Time Needed: 15 minutes
  • Difficulty Level: 2
  • Number of Portions: 4

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread from the loaf
  • 8 cheese slices
  • 4 sausages from the package
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt from the container
  • 2 tablespoons of mustard from the bottle
  • 2 tablespoons of Bresso spread
  • 2 tablespoons of Hermanns mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons of butter from the dish

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine the yogurt, mustard, Bresso spread, and Hermanns mayonnaise until smooth. Set aside.

  2. Preheat a skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter.

  3. Add the sausages and cook until browned and cooked through, about 7-10 minutes. Remove from skillet and slice each sausage lengthwise.

  4. Toast the bread slices in the same skillet or under a grill until golden brown.

  5. Spread the yogurt mustard mixture generously over one side of each toasted bread slice.

  6. Place a cheese slice on the spread side of each slice of bread.

  7. Top four bread slices with the sliced sausages.

  8. Finish by topping with another bread slice, with the cheese side facing down towards the sausage.

  9. Press down gently and serve warm.

Enjoy your Savory Sausage & Cheese Sandwich with Mustard Yogurt Spread!
