Creamy Sausage Rice Skillet

Recipe Details

  • Time Needed: 30 minutes
  • Difficulty Level: 3
  • Number of Portions: 4

Ingredients

  • 200g rice (adjust depending on appetite and serving size)
  • 4 sausages from the package
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt from the container
  • 4 tablespoons of cream cheese from the container
  • 2 tablespoons of Bresso spread
  • 2 tablespoons of butter from the dish
  • 2 tablespoons of sauce from the glass jar with the yellow cap
  • 4 slices of cheese from the pack

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions until tender, then set aside.

  2. Heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter.

  3. Add the sausages to the skillet and cook until browned and fully cooked, about 7-10 minutes. Remove sausages and slice them into bite-sized pieces.

  4. In the same skillet, add the Bresso spread, yogurt, cream cheese, and the sauce from the jar with the yellow cap. Stir until smooth and creamy.

  5. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix well, coating the rice with the creamy mixture.

  6. Gently fold in the sliced sausages and cheese slices, allowing the cheese to melt and mix with the creamy sauce.

  7. Cook for an additional 3-5 minutes or until everything is heated through. Adjust seasonings to taste if needed.

  8. Serve warm and enjoy your Creamy Sausage Rice Skillet!
