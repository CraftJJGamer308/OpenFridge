Creamy Sausage and Veggie Stir-Fry

Recipe Details

  • Time Needed: 25 minutes
  • Difficulty Level: 3
  • Number of Portions: 4

Ingredients We Already Have

  • 200g rice
  • 4 sausages from the package
  • 1/2 cup of yogurt from the container
  • 3 tablespoons of cream cheese from the container
  • 2 tablespoons of Bresso spread
  • 2 tablespoons of sauce from the glass jar with the red cap
  • 2 cheese slices
  • 2 tablespoons of butter from the dish

Ingredients to Buy

  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the rice according to package instructions until tender, then set aside.

  2. While the rice is cooking, heat a large skillet over medium heat and add 2 tablespoons of butter.

  3. Add the minced garlic and sliced onions to the skillet and sauté for 2-3 minutes until the onions are translucent.

  4. Add the sliced bell pepper and continue to sauté for another 3-4 minutes until the pepper is tender.

  5. Add the sausages to the skillet and cook until browned and fully cooked, about 7-10 minutes. Remove sausages and slice them into bite-sized pieces.

  6. In the same skillet, add the Bresso spread, yogurt, cream cheese, and the sauce from the glass jar with the red cap. Stir until smooth and creamy.

  7. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and mix well, coating the rice with the creamy mixture.

  8. Gently fold in the sliced sausages and cheese slices, allowing the cheese to melt into the sauce.

  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and cook for an additional 3-5 minutes or until everything is heated through.

  10. Serve warm and enjoy your Creamy Sausage and Veggie Stir-Fry!
