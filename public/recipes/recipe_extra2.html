Cheesy Sausage and Veggie Bake

Recipe Details

  • Time Needed: 40 minutes
  • Difficulty Level: 3
  • Number of Portions: 4

Ingredients We Already Have

  • 1/2 cup of yogurt from the container
  • 2 tablespoons of Bresso spread
  • 3 tablespoons of Hermanns mayonnaise
  • 4 slices of bread from the loaf
  • 4 eggs from the carton
  • 4 tablespoons of butter from the dish
  • 4 cheese slices
  • 4 sausages from the package
  • 2 tablespoons of mustard from the bottle
  • 2 tablespoons of tomato ketchup from the bottle
  • 2 tablespoons of sauce from the glass jar with the red cap

Ingredients to Buy

  • 1 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).

  2. In a skillet, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the sausages and cook until browned and fully cooked, about 7-10 minutes. Remove and slice into rounds.

  3. In the same skillet, add another 2 tablespoons of butter and sauté the zucchini, red bell pepper, and cherry tomatoes for about 5 minutes until just tender. Season with salt and pepper.

  4. In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, yogurt, Bresso spread, Hermanns mayonnaise, mustard, ketchup, and sauce from the glass jar with the red cap until well combined.

  5. Fold in the cooked vegetables and sliced sausages into the egg mixture.

  6. Cut the bread slices into cubes and add them to a greased baking dish. Pour the egg and sausage mixture over the bread cubes.

  7. Layer cheese slices on top of the mixture evenly.

  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the eggs are set and the top is golden brown.

  9. Let it cool slightly before serving your delicious Cheesy Sausage and Veggie Bake!
